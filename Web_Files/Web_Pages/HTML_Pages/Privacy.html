Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Eventra is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our platform.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal details such as name, email, and contact number during registration
  • Event registration history and preferences
  • Login credentials and session data

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To manage event registrations and user accounts
  • To send notifications and updates related to events
  • To improve platform functionality and user experience

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except when required by law or with your explicit consent.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

6. Cookies and Tracking

Eventra may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

7. Your Rights

  • You may request access to your personal data
  • You may update or correct your information
  • You may request deletion of your account and associated data

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@eventra.edu or visit our Contact page.
